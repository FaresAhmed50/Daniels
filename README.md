
# Demo Website

## Project Overview

This demo website serves as a sleek, modern portfolio or company landing page with clean, informative sections designed to impress potential clients or visitors. It showcases key metrics, introduces the team, provides contact details, and ensures smooth communication through a contact form. All sections are designed to offer an engaging, user-friendly experience.

---

## Features

### Statistics Section
Get a snapshot of your company's most impressive numbers. This section celebrates milestones such as the number of happy customers, completed projects, lines of code, and files downloaded. These figures reflect the company's achievements and successes in an easy-to-read format.

### Team Section
Meet the brains behind the operations! This section introduces team members, showcasing their roles, photos, and links to their social media profiles. It's a great way to connect with your audience and show off the talent within your organization.

### Contact Section
Stay connected! The contact section offers users a direct way to reach out through an easy-to-fill form. Along with this, you get quick access to the company’s physical address, email, and phone number. Whether it’s a query, feedback, or a business proposal, this section opens the door for communication.

### Footer
Wrap up your webpage with essential details in the footer. Here, you'll find the copyright notice, keeping the content protected and attributed to the creators. Simple and clean.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**:
  - HTML5: For structuring the content and layout.
  - CSS3: For styling the page and ensuring it looks great on all devices.
  - Bootstrap 5: To create a responsive, grid-based design that adjusts smoothly across devices.
  - FontAwesome: Adding a touch of style and functionality with its collection of icons.

## Installation & Setup

### Prerequisites

Ensure that you have a browser to view the demo and an internet connection to load external resources (e.g., FontAwesome, Bootstrap).

### Steps

1. **Clone the repository:**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/your-repo/demo-website.git
   cd demo-website
   ```

2. **Open the project in a browser:**
   - Open `index.html` directly in your browser or use any local server (e.g., [Live Server](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=ritwickdey.LiveServer)) to preview the website.
